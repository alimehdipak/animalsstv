Have you ever heard of Kangaroo? Of course? Anyway, that unique animal of Australia is too famous not to know about! How about a tree kangaroo? Never? Don\u2019t worry because it is totally understandable if you\u2019ve never heard of that elusive species\u2019s name.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Let us introduce to you this adorable animal! It contains many traits similar to Kangaroo, which explains its name, apart from the super fluffy body making it look like a cute bear.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

If you guess that these cute \u201cbears\u201d come from Australia, especially the northern regions, you\u2019re right. However, they may also be found in the mountainous and lowland forests of Papua New Guinea and Indonesia.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

As the name indicates, tree-kangaroos reside in the trees. Their body has evolved with stronger forelimbs and shorter legs to adapt to their habitat.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

This beautiful species is so rare that most of us don\u2019t even know its name. Luckily, in 2016, a tree kangaroo was given birth in captivity for the first time after 36 years. This has fortified conservationists\u2019 hope to increase this species\u2019s population and protect it from extinction.<\/p>\n
