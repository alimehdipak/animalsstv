Not many of us think of adopting a wild puma, right? It\u2019s a majestic cat that belongs to the wild. Scroll to see the story about Messi the puma and the family.
\n<\/p>\n

Messi the puma
\nYou can also find them in zoos and sanctuaries. But bring them home is challenging. You have to domesticize this big cat, making them become a house cat.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Mariya and Aleksandr Dmitriev, a couple from Russia did an extra thing. They adopted a rescued puma from the Saransk Zoo in Penza.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

The couple first met Messi (name of the puma) when he was only eight-months-old. The wild cat was struggling with health issues then. They loved the puma at the first sight.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Aleksandr has always dreamed of owning a big cat, a lynx, for instance. When he and his wife, Mariya spotted the puma at the zoo, they thought it was destiny. They want to give him a home.
\nTherefore, they decided to take a negotiation with the zoo to buy the puma. Luckily, the zoo agreed.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Aleksandr\u2019s dream has come true. He gets the puma as a family member.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

The couple truly loves the wild cat. They give Messi needed medical attention and proper care. They also take him for walk two times a day.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

\u201cHe is like a dog. We started taking him for a walk, step by step. The animal hadn\u2019t been really active before, Aleksandr said in an interview with RT, \u201cNow we walk a lot twice a day, as it\u2019s supposed to be. It doesn\u2019t differ much from owning a dog in this sense.\u201d<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Mariya and Aleksandr also find a dog training school for their puma. Messi is kind, playful, friendly, and sometimes, causes small naughty things.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Adopting a wild animal is not an easy task but the couple has been doing really great. The three are living happily in their apartment.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

The wild cat also owns an Instagram account with more than 600k followers. He is also a star on Youtube with over 1780k subscribers. Wild animals can be adorable and affectionate, guys.<\/p>\n
