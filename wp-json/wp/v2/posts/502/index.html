Many photographers search a lifetime for one special shot but not all of them could be lucky enough to find just what they have been longing for. Some people, however, don\u2019t need to search at all. The exceptional moment simply comes to them naturally and all they need to do is just seizing the opportunity. That\u2019s it.
\nPhotographer Mads Nordsveen from Oslo was out hiking with his friends in Norway when a fascinating being caught his eyes: it was an all-white reindeer calf, wandering on the thick snow. To be honest, he almost disappeared into that white snowy background, but it\u2019s still hard not to notice such a gorgeous creature.<\/p>\n


\n\u201cI was walking in the mountains looking for nice landscapes for my travel photography when out of nowhere I saw this wonderful little creature,\u201d Nordsveen recalled. \u201cHe came very close to me and we looked at each other straight in the eyes.\u201d<\/p>\n

Despite his apparent camouflage, the baby reindeer wasn\u2019t shy in front of humans at all. He had no intention of running away or attacking the stranger. Needless to say, that little boy immediately captured Nordsveen\u2019s heart with his out-going and cheerful nature. He didn\u2019t mind posing a bit for the camera either.
\n\u201cHe was quite relaxed when he saw that I was calm and friendly,\u201d said the 24-year-old photographer. \u201cIt was almost as if he posed for the camera. He was very curious and fun. Like a little explorer.\u201d<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Image credits: Mads Nordsveen \/ Caters News
\nTheir joyous meeting didn\u2019t last really long though. The calf\u2019s mother appeared shortly afterward and they quickly parted.<\/p>\n

\u201cAfter some minutes the mother of the baby reindeer came out of trees just behind,\u201d he said. \u201cHe walked around for some minutes before running back to his mother.<\/p>\n

However, Nordsveen would never forget that magnificent encounter.<\/p>\n

\u201cIt was very magical and a fairy tale moment,\u201d the man continued. \u201cMaybe one day we\u2019ll meet again.\u201d
\n<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

White reindeers are extremely rare in the wild. In spite of the perfectly white coats, it\u2019s not hard to see that they aren\u2019t albino since these charming creatures still have some dark pigmentation in their eyes and antlers.
