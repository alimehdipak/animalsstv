Like the people in the list we have collected below, no one in this world can look like them totally. #1. Biggest Hands<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Tarik is an Indian boy. Because his hands grow to 12 INCHES, he is called the \u2018devil\u2019 by local villages who think that his big hands are the result of a curse. He has had big hands since birth. Some physicians believe that he may have Elephant Foot illness, but this has not been confirmed.<\/p>\n

#2. The Real-Life Rapunzel
\n<\/p>\n

Asha Mandela, a self-described \u201cBlack Rapunzel,\u201d holds the Guinness World Record for having the longest dreadlocks ever. The length of Mandela\u2019s dreadlocks is approximately 19 feet, 6 inches.
\nMandela has been growing her hair for more than 25 years and has a complex routine for caring for her priceless tresses. For each wash, she begins by using more than six bottles of shampoo, which is followed by a lengthy drying procedure that can take up to two days to complete.<\/p>\n

#3. Conjoined Twins<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Conjoined twins who had a one-in-a-million chance of surviving at birth are now adults and teachers! Conjoined twins Abigail and Brittany Hensel are those who are physically joined at birth and have their own hearts, lungs, stomachs, and spinal cords.<\/p>\n


\n#4. The Human Barbie
\nBecause looking like a real-life Barbie doll, Ukrainian model and influencer Valeria Lukyanova is very famous. She appears to be the most convincing real-life Barbie in the world, in some strange way.<\/p>\n

#5. World\u2019s Widest Hips<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

According to Guinness World Records, Mikel Ruffinelli, an American, has the widest hips in the world. Ruffinelli\u2019s waist is barely 3\u20194\u2033, but her hips have an amazing circumference of eight feet.<\/p>\n

In her early 20s, Mikel was a normal-sized young lady. However, she didn\u2019t gain 56 pounds after delivering her first kid until she was 22 years old. Her weight and hips kept increasing as her daughters followed.<\/p>\n

#6. World\u2019s Hairiest Man<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Zhenhuan is the hairiest guy ever known and his hair covers 96% of his body. Therefore, Zhenhuan is also referred to as \u201cthe hair boy.\u201d He underwent laser surgery in 2009 to get rid of all of his body hair.<\/p>\n

#7. The tallest man in the world
\n<\/p>\n

Sultan Kosen is the tallest living person in the world at 251 cm, according to the Guinness Book of World Records. Pituitary gigantism is the cause of Sultan\u2019s height. Because of the situation he is in, he is forced to leave school in the middle of the academic year. On the bright side, Kosen was able to assist his parents with a variety of domestic chores that kids his age often couldn\u2019t handle, including hanging curtains or replacing lightbulbs<\/p>\n

#8. The shortest man in the world<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Chandra Bahadur Dangi, a native of Nepal, is the smallest individual to have ever been included in the history of Guinness World Records when it comes to height. Dangi, who is just 54.61 cm tall, won the contest against Gul Mohammed to become the \u201cWorld\u2019s Shortest Man.\u201d<\/p>\n

#9. World\u2019s shortest woman<\/p>\n


\nJyoti Amge of Nagpur, India, is the smallest woman in the world, standing at under 25 inches tall. She plans to attend college and hopes to become a successful actress. She is just nine pounds heavier than when she was born at her current weight of 12 pounds.<\/p>\n

She has even been in a number of well-known television programs, like Bigg Boss and American Horror Story (an Indian adaptation of the reality show Big Brother.)
\n#10. Plastic Surgery Aficionado<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Jocelyn Wildenstein, known as Catwoman because of her feline-like appearance and obsession with cosmetic surgery, has had more operations than she cares to admit or count.<\/p>\n

#11. Papa Smurf<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Paul Karason is better known as Papa Smurf because of his blue skin. He wasn\u2019t born with this skin tone at first. Paul\u2019s use of colloidal silver to treat his skin issue has changed the hue of his skin.<\/p>\n

The medication\u2019s silver combined with sunshine to produce argyria, a word used in medicine to describe blue skin. But despite that, he continued to use ingesting silver. Karason consumed silver till he died because he thought it would heal his acid reflux, arthritis, and other illnesses.<\/p>\n

#12. The world\u2019s most tattooed woman<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Julia Gnuse, also known as \u201cThe Irvine Walker\u201d or \u201cThe Illustrated Lady,\u201d holds the Guinness World Record for having the most tattoos on her body, about 95%. Her tattoos are ranging from her favorite cartoons to actors to even a self-portrait.<\/p>\n

#13. World\u2019s Strongest Man<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Zydrnas Savickas, who is regarded as the greatest strength athlete of all time, has won nearly every significant strongman tournament in history. For instance, Savickas has four times been crowned the World\u2019s Strongest Man.<\/p>\n

#14. Elephant Legs<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

British lady Mandy Sellars\u2019 legs simply keep getting bigger. Despite having a rare genetic mutation, Sellars jokingly describes herself as a \u201cpart-time television star.\u201d To put it simply, she is immobile due to her disease, which results in excessive development in both of her legs.<\/p>\n

Sellars thinks she is much more than her medical condition, thus she refuses to be seen through that lens. She actually tells her story to inform and advocate for individuals who experience similar medical difficulties.<\/p>\n

#15. World\u2019s Longest Tongue<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Nick Stoeberl has a very tonge. He can easily lick his nose and elbows. He even made an attempt to impress America\u2019s Got Talent judges by drawing a heart with his tongue.<\/p>\n

#16. World\u2019s Dirtiest Man<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Amoo Hadji apparently doesn\u2019t understand the adage \u201ccleanliness is next to godliness,\u201d since he is everything from clean. Hadji, regarded as the dirtiest man in the world, is said to have gone more than 65 years without taking a shower.<\/p>\n

Hadji has developed some really peculiar hobbies to keep himself company, aside from not showering, such as eating corpses and smoking elephant dung. We\u2019re shocked, to be honest.<\/p>\n

#17. World\u2019s bendiest woman
\n<\/p>\n

The world\u2019s most flexible lady, Julia Zlata, can effortlessly and gracefully twist her body into any position.<\/p>\n

In addition to having elastic limbs by birth, Zlata works hard to hone the skill that has made her famous. It appears that Zlata has no intention of slowing down any time soon. I commend her!<\/p>\n

#18. The Jaguar Woman<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Mexican lawyer Mar\u00eda Jos\u00e9 Cristerna M\u00e9ndez is also known as The Vampire Woman or \u2014 as she prefers, The Jaguar Woman. Cristerna conveys a powerful message against domestic violence using her body as a medium.<\/p>\n

In addition to having physical alterations including dental implants, a split tongue, expanded ears, tattooed eyes, and more, Maria has tattoos covering 96% of her body. Cristerna has a clothing brand and her own tattoo parlor in addition to being a criminal defense attorney.<\/p>\n

#19. World\u2019s Largest Mouth<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

For possessing the widest mouth in the whole globe, Francisco Domingo Joaquim now holds the Guinness World Record. The width of Joaquim\u2019s mouth is 6.7 inches. This indicates that Joaquim has no trouble swallowing a can of Pepsi sideways.<\/p>\n

Joaquim can consume more food than a group of people together, which allows him to eat more quickly than other individuals. He\u2019s giving the term \u201cbig mouth\u201d a completely new interpretation.<\/p>\n

#20. World\u2019s Smallest Waist<\/p>\n
