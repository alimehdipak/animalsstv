Pets are part of the family. It is difficult when we have to separate from them, and when this man found out that his dog would not live long, he decided to take him in a wheelchair to his favorite place for the last time.<\/p>\n

Saying goodbye to a longtime pet is like saying goodbye to a family member. These emotional photos show how the owner of the dog last decided to drive his pet in a stroller to his favorite place in the mountains.<\/p>\n

During their ten-year stay together, the dog Labrador Monti enjoyed exploring the hills and mountain peaks with its owner Carlos Fresco. Their favorite place was the peaks of Penn and Fen in Wales, where they went together for years.<\/p>\n

Carlos says he knew their long-term friendship ended after Monty was re-diagnosed with cancer. The little hero struggled with a serious illness for 18 months, and his owner, wanting to please him, decided to take him to his favorite place for the last time.<\/p>\n

Since Monty was very exhausted from the disease and could not climb the steep paths on his own, Carlos drove him in a construction cart this time.
