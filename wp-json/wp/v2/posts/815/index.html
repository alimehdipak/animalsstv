A wolf with a heart-shaped face? You may think that this never happens but it does. The unique creature is snapped on camera by Finnish photographer Niko Pekonen.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Mother nature can always keep humans amazed at the distinct beauty of wild animals. They were born with unique markings, making them special in their community.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

The heart-shaped face wolf in the photo has shaken the world. Niko spotted and snapped awesome shoots of the wolf in the North European wilderness.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

The exclusive creature is a grey wolf (Canis lupus), one of the largest land predators in Eurasia and North America. It isn\u2019t much different from any other wolves of its species, except for his face. Its grey coat comes nearly to its cheeks, forming a heart shape for its face. This unique born marking makes the wolf stand out in its colony.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

The wolf looks extremely beautiful.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Niko is so lucky that he had an intimate encounter with it. To any photographer, take once in lifetime photos is like winning a lottery ticket. It will be the greatest success in their photography life.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

If you fall in love with this heart-shaped face wolf, don\u2019t forget to share it with family and friends. Nature is amazing!<\/p>\n
