Meet Dick van Duijn, a 34-year-old Dutch photographer who is good at capturing the beauty of nature and wildlife. His photos are so wonderful that people from around the world love his work and eagerly follow him on Instagram to update his latest photos every day.<\/p>\n

Recently, he captured some heartwarming pictures of a squirrel gasping at the sheer beauty of a yellow daisy. These pictures quickly went viral and people can\u2019t get enough of them. In the photos, the squirrel stands on its hind legs, reaches up to the daisy and sniffs with its eyes closed. It\u2019s so adorable!<\/p>\n

Duijn says he was captured this precious moment on a short trip to Austria made specifically to photograph the ground squirrels on June 5, 2019. \u201cThere were a lot of curious squirrels, so it was not hard to photograph them, but to capture one with a flower like this took a lot of patience and shots. The moment the squirrel smelled the flower and planted his face in the flower, I knew this was the picture of a lifetime,\u201d he said.<\/p>\n

The world isn\u2019t too great a place right now, but thankfully there are still a few things that can bring a bit more joy to your day. Just take a look at Duijn\u2019s most beautiful animal photos below, you will feel good all day. So scroll down, enjoy the gorgeous photos and don\u2019t forget to share this post with your friends to brighten up their day.<\/p>\n
